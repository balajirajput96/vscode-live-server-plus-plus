## Interview Preparation Content

### Common Questions for QC/QA Apprentice
1. What is cGMP and why is it important in pharmaceutical manufacturing?
2. Explain ALCOA+ principles in data integrity.
3. What is the difference between QC and QA?
4. Describe the importance of documentation in pharmaceutical operations.
5. What are deviation and CAPA in quality systems?
6. How would you handle an OOS (Out of Specification) result?
7. What safety precautions would you take in a laboratory?
8. Describe your understanding of BMR and BPR.
9. How do you ensure accuracy in data recording?
10. Why are you interested in the pharmaceutical industry?

### Production Trainee Questions
1. What is 5S methodology and its importance in manufacturing?
2. Explain the concept of line clearance.
3. What is IPC (In-Process Control) and why is it important?
4. Describe different dosage forms you know about.
5. What is the role of EHS in pharmaceutical manufacturing?
6. How would you handle a batch failure situation?
7. What is reconciliation in production?
8. Explain the importance of material status labeling.
9. What are the different phases of tablet manufacturing?
10. How do you ensure compliance with SOPs?

### Lab Technician Questions
1. Describe proper pipetting techniques.
2. What is TAT (Turn Around Time) and its importance?
3. How do you handle sample contamination?
4. What are quality control measures in laboratory testing?
5. Explain the importance of sample labeling and tracking.
6. How do you maintain laboratory equipment?
7. What is the difference between accuracy and precision?
8. Describe biosafety levels and precautions.
9. How do you handle laboratory waste disposal?
10. What are the common laboratory errors and how to prevent them?

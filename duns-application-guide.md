## 🎯 D-U-N-S Number क्या है?

**D-U-N-S (Data Universal Numbering System)** एक unique 9-digit identifier है जो businesses को globally identify करने के लिए use होता है। यह Dun & Bradstreet द्वारा maintain किया जाता है।

### क्यों जरूरी है?
- Google Play Console developer account verification
- Apple Developer Program enrollment  
- Government contracts और tenders
- International business transactions
- Credit और financial services

## 🚨 Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Application Rejected
**Reasons:**
- Incomplete documentation
- Address verification failure
- Incorrect business information

**Solutions:**
- Resubmit with complete documents
- Verify address details
- Contact D&B support for clarification

### Issue: Delayed Processing
**Reasons:**
- High application volume
- Document verification pending
- Additional information required

**Solutions:**
- Follow up regularly
- Provide requested information promptly
- Consider expedited processing

### Issue: Duplicate D-U-N-S Found
**Reasons:**
- Institution already has D-U-N-S
- Similar name/address in database
- Previous application exists

**Solutions:**
- Search existing D-U-N-S database
- Contact D&B to claim existing number
- Provide differentiation documents
